Introduction

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an artificial intelligence chatbot created by OpenAI. It can generate human-like text based on user prompts. People use it to answer questions, explain concepts, write drafts, summarize information, create code, and hold conversations.

How It Works in Simple Terms

ChatGPT does not think like a human. Instead, it uses patterns learned from large amounts of text to predict and generate useful responses. This makes it powerful, but it also means the system can sometimes produce answers that sound correct even when they are wrong.

Why It Is Useful

  • Explains difficult topics in simple language
  • Helps with brainstorming and writing support
  • Assists with coding and troubleshooting
  • Can improve productivity in many fields

Why It Is Risky

  • Can create convincing misinformation
  • May reflect bias from training data
  • Can encourage cheating or overreliance
  • Raises privacy and job displacement concerns

Why Our Team Chose ChatGPT

We chose ChatGPT because it is not a future idea anymore. It is already being used by students, teachers, businesses, healthcare workers, and everyday users. Its benefits and risks are happening at the same time, which makes it an important technology to study through ethical and social lenses.
